Projects

Rail-Way

I led this project as part of a four-person team for our EvolveU project 3. The country of Nigeria has recently been developing its rail and metro network. Unfortunately, train schedules are only available as PDF documents with no digital solution. Rail-Way attempts to resolve this issue by providing a dynamic rail schedule presented as either the next departing train for a route or a route's weekly schedule. Also, the application provides travelers with a basic trip planner.

Dynamic To Do List

I built this document for my front-end web development program at the University of Calgary. The to-do list allows the creation of tasks via a form with DOM manipulation through JavaScript. The list ultilizes local storage for persistance, drag-and-drop as a means of re-ordering list items, and prototypal inheritance to create different task categories
